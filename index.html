Happiness & Wellbeing trends: Australia vs the Extremes


The Gallup World Poll (GWP) conducts surveys and collects data from over 160 countries.

One of their publications is the World Happiness Report (WHR).


In this Narrative Visualisation, I use the WHR 2024 dataset to explore the following questions:

  • What were the most and least happy countries in 2023?
  • Where does Australia sit in relation to them?
  • What trends are visible over time?
  • How do other wellbeing-related indicators compare?
